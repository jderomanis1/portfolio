01 / The Record

The Delivery Ledger

Every entry closes with the same line: what value actually landed. Expand any record.

The first chapter of a 25-plus year career: hands-on engineering and systems work that grew into technical leadership. I learned delivery from the inside, writing the code, running the systems, and owning the outcomes, before I ever coached a team or advised an executive. That foundation is why my consulting has always been grounded in what actually ships.

A practitioner's foundation: advice backed by the scars of having built and operated production systems firsthand.

More than a decade across the enterprise consulting spectrum, from delivery lead to principal, serving Fortune-scale clients in entertainment, hospitality, energy, equipment services, and healthcare. Both sides of the table: I've sold the work, scoped the work, staffed the work, and been personally accountable for the P&L of the work landing. The three engagements below are drawn from this era.

A repeatable pattern clients renewed year after year: walk into their hardest delivery problem, leave behind a working platform, a stronger team, and a cleaner cost structure.

Led the consulting team that launched Ticketmaster's resale platform, a ground-up build of capabilities that didn't exist anywhere in the company: interactive seat mapping, virtual seat views, and fan-to-fan listing. Delivered under direct CTO visibility, then drove refinement and enhancement cycles post-launch through 2012 and beyond.

Multimillion-dollar resale revenue stream unlocked; net-new product line for the market leader in live entertainment.

Drove a large-scale redesign of the Choice Privileges promotional engine, introducing faster point-earning structures and mid-week stay incentives to shift booking behavior where hotels needed it most. Worked shoulder-to-shoulder with Choice Hotels EMEA chief executive David Beers to align program mechanics with revenue strategy across markets.

Loyalty program re-architected to drive mid-week occupancy and accelerate member engagement across a global hotel network.

Led back-to-back modernization efforts for a major Arizona client: full SharePoint platform overhauls, followed by the launch of a custom application built with Xamarin that put integrated workflows directly in customers' hands instead of behind internal process walls.

Measurably stronger customer experience: a modernized collaboration platform plus a custom app with workflows integrated end to end, cutting manual handoffs out of the process.

Stood up the Agile Center of Excellence for one of the largest health services companies in the world, building the training curriculum, coaching practice, and governance patterns that made Agile and SAFe stick beyond the workshop room. Best practice wasn't a binder; it was enforced in how teams actually ran. Retained directly by the client after the consulting engagement ended, converting a 12-year consulting relationship into a multi-year direct role.

An enterprise CoE that outlived its founders: agile practice institutionalized across the organization, with faster flow and less rework as the operating norm.

Pioneered enterprise Claude adoption with CIO-level sponsorship: authored the security risk analysis, licensing strategy, and rollout plan end to end. Shipped a production AI delivery intelligence platform (Claude + Atlassian MCP + live Jira, Confluence, and Slack data) that replaced roughly 4 hours of manual sprint reporting per cycle with on-demand executive artifacts. Corrected velocity reporting that had been understated by up to 60%, restoring leadership trust in program metrics. Now leading an approved pilot where Claude and Claude Code run dev, QE, and product management on a live reporting migration, backed by a hard-savings cost model and sponsor-commissioned adversarial spot checks.

Enterprise-approved AI-native delivery pilot with a hard-dollar savings case: contract spend redeployed, roadmap dates beaten by design, and a playbook built to scale on success.
